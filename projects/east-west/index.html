The pipeline

From trending in China
to published in English.

A fully automated pipeline — four stages, zero manual bottlenecks.

01
Discover

Monitors Douyin, Bilibili, Weibo, and Xiaohongshu for trending content using engagement signals and velocity metrics.

02
Analyze

AI extracts key themes, cultural context, and narrative structure. Determines what makes the content resonate and why.

03
Create

Generates original commentary, reaction scripts, and summary narration. Transformative by design — not a repost, a perspective.

04
Publish

Schedules and distributes across YouTube, TikTok, and Instagram Reels. Credits and links the original creator every time.

Why it works

Built to be a curator,
not a bot farm.

🔍
Trend Intelligence

Tracks velocity and engagement signals across Chinese platforms to surface content before it peaks — giving the curator a first-mover advantage in the Western market.

✍️
Transformative Output

Every piece of content is original commentary, reaction, or summary — not a copy. This is culturally additive and protects under transformative use principles.

🤝
Creator Attribution

Original creators are credited and linked in every post. The pipeline treats Chinese creators as collaborators, not sources to extract from.

🎭
Persona-First Design

The curator is the brand. The AI is the engine behind the scenes. Audiences follow a trusted human voice, not an automation tool.

⚡
End-to-End Automation

From discovery to publish, the pipeline runs autonomously. The curator reviews and approves — volume and speed without proportional effort.

📊
Performance Feedback Loop

Engagement data feeds back into the discovery model. Over time, the pipeline gets sharper at predicting what Western audiences want to see.
