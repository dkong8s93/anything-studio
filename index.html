// 01 — Media & Content
East → West
AI-Powered Content Pipeline

Chinese social platforms surface viral, culturally rich content that Western audiences never see. East → West is an automated pipeline that discovers trending videos, generates original commentary and summary content around them, and publishes under a curator persona — bridging cultures, not scraping them.

  • Discovers trending content across Chinese platforms in real time
  • Generates transformative commentary, reactions, and summaries via AI
  • Credits and links original creators — curator, not a bot farm
  • You become the trusted translator persona for Western audiences

// 02 — Real Estate & AI
Red-Flag
Disclosure Intelligence Agent

A 200-page disclosure package lands in a buyer's inbox. Nobody reads it. Red-Flag does. Drop a property link or PDF and Claude's vision + long-context models instantly surface what matters — so buyers make informed decisions instead of just trusting their agent.

  • Ingests messy PDFs: handwritten notes, old inspections, HOA bylaws
  • Flags material issues — roof age, foundation repairs, restriction clauses
  • Mobile-first, results in seconds, plain-English output
  • Powered by Claude vision + long-context document understanding
